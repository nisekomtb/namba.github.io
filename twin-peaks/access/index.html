Trail Access

Please follow these access instructions to ensure a smooth and respectful experience while visiting the park.

  1. Starting Point: Yamada Jinja (Shrine). Begin your journey at the Yamada Jinja, also known as the Yamada Shrine entrance.
  2. Access Path:
    • Bikers are prohibited from using the sando (gravel path) that goes under the silver tori gate.
    • Instead, bikers must use the access gravel path located to the right of the sando.
  3. Crossing the Sando: When you reach the end of the access gravel path and need to cross the sando:
    • Dismount your bike.
    • Walk your bike across the short distance of the sando.
    • This is to respect the pathway to the gods and ensure the sanctity of the shrine area.
  4. Enjoy the Twin Peaks Bike Park:
    • Once you have crossed the sando, you are free to enjoy the bike park to its fullest extent.
    • Please adhere to all park rules and regulations for your safety and the safety of others.

Thank you for your cooperation in respecting the cultural and religious significance of the Yamada Jinja Shrine area. Enjoy your ride at Twin Peaks Bike Park!
