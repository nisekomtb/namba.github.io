Twin Peaks Bike Park

A free community bike park

The Twin Peaks Bike Park in Niseko, Japan is a world-class mountain biking destination designed in collaboration with renowned trail building company Allegra of Switzerland. The bike park offers free access to the public, and trails for all ages and abilities.

Whilst the trails are free to members of the general public, guides, instructors and tour operators must purchase commercial operating passes. For more information on these commercial passes, click here.
