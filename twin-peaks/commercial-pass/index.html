Twin Peaks Bike Park: Commercial Pass

{% comment %}

For the 2024 season commercial passes are being introduced as part of a closed pilot program, and participation is limited to trail sponsors, and other special sponsors.

{% endcomment %}

NAMBA is testing a commercial operator pass for instructors, guides, tours, and other commercial operators looking to operate within the Twin Peaks Bike Park. As a non-profit organization, NAMBA strives to maintain our trails for the enjoyment of all users, and this program is a positive step towards ensuring their sustainability and quality.

Commercial operators conducting instruction, guiding, tours or providing any other services within the bike park must have a Commercial Operator Pass to operate within the park. Violations may result in suspensions or bans from the park for staff and/or the entire organization.

Contact NAMBA to learn more.
