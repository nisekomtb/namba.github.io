--- title: Become a Partner description: NAMBA is a grassroots non-profit organization located in the international resort town of Niseko, Japan. Sponsorship and partnership help support mountain biking in Niseko and shape the future of the sport in Asia. ---

Tell your story, shape ours

NAMBA is fueled by grants, donations & partnerships. At the risk of sounding hyperbolic, trails & bike parks like the free & public Twin Peaks Bike Park cannot exist without financial support.
Through sponsoring NAMBA, your brand or business will advance the sport of mountain biking and increase its accessibility in underdeveloped local, national and regional markets. In doing so, your business will become linked to Niseko - a door to Japan & markets like China, Singapore and Malaysia - and will shape the story of NAMBA & of mountain biking in Japan.
Do good for the community & for your business: grow core markets and tap into new ones. Tell your brand's story all while supporting a grassroots nonprofit initiative.

Partnership Benefits

Exposure

Your brand or business will be recognized as a supporter of NAMBA no matter how much you sponsor or donate.

Improved infrastructure

The infrastructure we will be contributing to will include trails, skills parks, viewing stations, walking trails, running trails, signage, and snowshoe trails.

Tangible assets

Your contribution will go towards something that will continue to be used for years to come, something that will grow, evolve and improve.

Participation

All members will receive information on dig days, ways to participate and contribute.

An increase to summer & shoulder season tourism

The use of the trails in spring can start a short time after the snow melts on each zone or area and carry on until the snow fall in late Autumn.
