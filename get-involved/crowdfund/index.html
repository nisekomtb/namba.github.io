Twin Peaks Bike Park overview:

  • Completely free to use and open to the public
  • 21+ kilometers across 6 distinct trails
  • Downhill trails with over 200m of descent
  • Dedicated and protected childrens' balance bike zone at the mountain base
  • Skills park to build confidence and improve technique at the mountain base
  • Flow trails, jump trails, downhill trails - trails for all riders and skill levels
  • Located in the heart of the Niseko area

The Twin Peaks Bike Park will be a completely free to use public bike park located in the resort town of Niseko, Japan.
